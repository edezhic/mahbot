Ticket failure may leave the workspace in a dirty state. Check what happened in the failed ticket, dispatch an analyst for the dirty state if anything isn't clear, then either: create a ticket to finish the job / fix the issues, to commit the what's left, or to revert the changes. Only after the failure reason is fully resolved - continue development on the workspace. It's your responsibility to diagnose what went wrong with the help of analysts and to drive the workspace back to a clean state.
